Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How do I get started with SmartPOS?
Simply click the Get Started button, create your account, and you can start using SmartPOS immediately. Our onboarding wizard will guide you through the setup process.
Can I use SmartPOS on multiple devices?
Yes! SmartPOS is cloud-based and works on any device with a web browser. Your data syncs in real-time across all devices.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and regular backups to ensure your data is always safe and protected.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
